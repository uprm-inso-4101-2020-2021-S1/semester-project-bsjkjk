Autoridad de Acueductos y Alcantarillados

The "Autoridad de Acueductos y Alcantarillados" (AAA) is the main water and sewage corporation in Puerto Rico which provides its inhabitants with service of potable water, sanitary sewerage and any other services related to water supply.


This agency is responsible for repairing faults related to any of the following:

  • Water Quality
  • Water Pressure
  • Water Resource Management
