Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


How do I report a fault?

To report a fault you will first need to create an account. Once you have created an account you will have access to the report a fault feature. First you will need to choose a fault type depending on your issue, then below you will describe the fault and its location. Once you have described the fault click the submit button and your report will appear.

How do I create an account?

To create and account go to the upper menu and click Sign Up. Enter a unique username, your email, and a password. Once all your information is entered, click the Sign Up button.

How can I find information or get in contact directly with a specific agency?

To find information of a specific agency, go to our homepage and search for the Contact Information section. This section contains the list of agencies. Click on the desired agency and you will be able to see information about the agency, including phone numbers, links to their website and social medias.

How do I create a fault report for an agency not demonstrated in the available options?

To report a fault from an agency not in the available options, follow the same process of creating a normal report but in the fault type choose the Other option. It is recommended to include the agency related to the fault and be more specific with details in your description.

What is the vouch button for?

The vouch button works as an upvote system, so other users can confirm if the reported fault is true. A user can vouch your report as a confirmation that they have indeed been witnesses to the fault in the specified location. The vouch system can also be used so different users do not have to report the same fault multiple times.
