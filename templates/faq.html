Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


How do I create an account?

To create an account go to the upper menu and click Sign Up. Enter a unique username, your email, and a password. Once all your information is entered, click the Sign Up button.

How do I report a fault?

To submit a fault report you will need to create an account. To create a report, choose a fault type depending on your issue then below you will provide information about the fault and its location. After, click the submit button and your report will appear.

How can I find information or get in contact directly with a specific agency?

To find the information go to our homepage and search for the Contact Information section which contains a list of agencies. Click on the desired agency to see information about them, including phone numbers, links to their website and social medias.

How do I create a fault report for an agency not demonstrated in the available options?

If the agency is not in the available options, follow the same process of reporting a fault but in the fault type choose the "Other" option. It is recommended to include the agency related to the fault and be more specific in your description.

What is the vouch button for?

The vouch button works as an upvote system. Other users can vouch your report to confirm that they have been witnesses to the fault in the specified location. It is also used so different users do not have to report the same fault multiple times.

How to check report status?

All reports have a status, either RESOLVED or UNRESOLVED shown under them. You can change your reports' status in My Reports page.
