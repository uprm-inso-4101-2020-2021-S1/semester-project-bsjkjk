Autoridad de Energía Eléctrica

The Puerto Rico Electric Power Authority or "Autoridad de Energía Eléctrica" is the electric power company owned by the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico. It is the main electrical service provider on the island.


This agency is responsible for repairing faults related to any of the following:

  • Power Distribution
  • Electricity Generation
  • Power Transmition
