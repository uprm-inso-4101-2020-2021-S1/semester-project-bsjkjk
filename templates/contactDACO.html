Departamento de Asuntos del Consumidor

The Department of Consumer Affairs (DACO) is an executive department of the government of Puerto Rico responsible for protecting and defending consumers' interest on the island.


This agency is responsible for helping with faults related to any of the following:

  • False Advertisements
  • Price of Items
  • Inflationary Trends
  • Deceptive Services
