Departamento de Transportación y Obras Públicas

The Department of Transportation and Public Works (DTOP) is an executive department of the government of Puerto Rico responsible for regulating and administering public infrastructures and transportation systems on the island.


This agency is responsible for helping with faults related to any of the following:

  • Public Roads
  • Driver's License
  • Vehicle Registration
